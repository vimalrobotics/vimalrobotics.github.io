## Overview
An IoT system that enables remote control of a PC power switch using voice commands.
The system emphasized electrical safety and isolation.

## Architecture
- ESP8266 D1 Mini
- Optocoupler-based electrical isolation
- Google Assistant integration

## Engineering Considerations
- Safe interfacing with high-voltage systems
- Network reliability
- Fail-safe switching

## Key Learnings
- Electrical isolation is critical in IoT automation
- Consumer IoT systems must be designed defensively
