## Overview
A bio-inspired adaptive gripper designed for handling irregular objects.
The design combined Fin Ray compliant structures with suction and electrostatic braking.

## Design Highlights
- Fin Ray inspired flexible structure
- Hybrid suction gripping
- Electrostatic locking mechanism

## Industrial Relevance
- Pick and place
- Palletizing
- Irregular object handling

## Key Learnings
- Compliance improves adaptability but complicates control
- Mechanical intelligence reduces sensing requirements
