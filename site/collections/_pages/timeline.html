2018 — Foundations in Electronics & Actuation

Began hands-on exploration in electronics and robotics through basic servo-driven mechanisms and sensor-based control systems. Early projects focused on understanding power management, actuator limitations, and mechanical improvisation using recycled materials.

2019 — Embedded Systems & IoT Integration

Transitioned toward embedded system reliability and safety. Developed IoT-enabled control systems using ESP8266, incorporating electrical isolation and cloud-based voice control.

2020 — Human Interaction & Embedded Intelligence

Worked on embedded vision systems and human-centered automation. Designed a face-detection-based welcome system with text-to-speech interaction aimed at stress reduction in public and workplace environments.

2021 — Mechatronics & Bio-Inspired Design

Focused on mechanical intelligence and adaptive manipulation. Designed a bio-inspired adaptive vacuum gripper integrating compliant Fin Ray structures, suction mechanisms, and electrostatic braking for industrial pick-and-place tasks.

2022 — Robotics Systems & Competitive Engineering

Participated in large-scale robotics competitions, developing a crane hook tracking and positioning system using a flight controller, wireless ultrasonic sensing, and a custom ground station interface. Reached the final round of the Techgium competition.

2023 — Autonomous Robotics & Research

Final-year research project focused on assistive robotics. Developed an autonomous towing robot for wheelchair assistance using ROS, navigation stacks, and real-world deployment constraints. This work resulted in an IEEE research publication.

2024 — Vision-Guided Robotics & System Integration

Expanded work in perception-driven manipulation and human-centric IoT systems. Developed a vision-guided SCARA robot using AprilTag-based feedback and inverse kinematics, alongside a comprehensive elderly monitoring system integrating physiological sensing and cloud alerts.

2025 — Advanced Robotics Platforms & Open-Source Education

Currently focused on scalable robotics platforms, advanced control techniques, and open-source educational robots. Ongoing work includes a laptop-based robotics platform designed to lower the barrier for robotics education and experimentation.
