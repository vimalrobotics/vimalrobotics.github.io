I am a Robotics Engineer with hands-on experience in the design, development, and deployment of autonomous robotic systems in real-world environments. My work spans the full robotics stack, with a strong focus on ROS 2-based software development, embedded systems, and hardware–software integration.

I specialize in building autonomous mobile robots (AMRs), assistive robotic systems, and intelligent IoT-enabled devices. My engineering approach blends control theory, perception, embedded programming, and system-level design to create reliable and scalable robotic solutions.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Robotics and Automation and a Diploma in Electronics and Robotics. During my academic and professional journey, I have worked on research-driven projects including an IEEE-published assistive robotic platform, vision-based robotic manipulation systems, and human-centric monitoring devices.

Currently, my interests lie in autonomous navigation, sensor fusion, embedded AI, and assistive robotics. I am particularly motivated by projects that bridge theoretical robotics research with practical, deployable systems that have a direct human impact.
